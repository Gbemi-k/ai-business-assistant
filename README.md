# ChatOrder

ChatOrder helps businesses take orders automatically through chat.

## What It Does

- Customers can ask questions and place orders through a simple chat link
- Orders are automatically saved in a business dashboard
- Business owners can manage products, services, and order status
- Customers can order without the business owner replying manually every time

## Why It Is Useful

Many businesses sell through WhatsApp, Instagram, or online chat and often:

- Miss messages
- Reply late
- Lose customers
- Struggle to track orders properly

ChatOrder helps by handling product questions, order collection, and dashboard tracking automatically.

## How It Works

1. A business creates an account
2. The business adds products or services
3. ChatOrder gives the business a customer ordering link
4. Customers open the link and chat with the AI assistant
5. Orders appear in the business dashboard
